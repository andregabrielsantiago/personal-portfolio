Project Description

The Institute of Business Management Department Shirt was designed to represent the department's identity, values, and collaborative spirit through a modern and professional aesthetic. Inspired by the concept of business as a living system, the design features a flowing DNA strand symbolizing the interconnectedness of different majors and a wolf representing unity, leadership, and excellence. The final design was officially approved and adopted by the department, and it has been worn by students since the 2024 batch, serving as a visual symbol of the Institute of Business Management community.

◄
DNA Strand
Represents the interconnectedness of all IBM majors — each strand a different discipline, woven into one unified system of growth.
◄
The Wolf
Embodies unity, leadership, and excellence — reflecting the strength of a community that moves forward together as one pack.
◄
Gold and Black Palette
Gold symbolizes academic excellence and ambition; black conveys professionalism, discipline, and prestige within the department.
◄
2024 Batch Legacy
Officially approved and adopted by the department, this design became the visual identity worn proudly since the 2024 student batch.
